The Math the best part!

To quantify how strong a card is, I did a couple things.

The Assumptions

  • Based on LLSIF version 4.0, because this version was game changing for JP and will be for EN.

  • Skill calculations are based off of a 125 second, 550 note long song with 65 star notes, assuming 85% Perfect Notes/15% Great Notes and Full Combo.

    The closest in-game variants to this theoretical song are EX Music Start (551 notes/124 seconds), Junai Lens (550 notes/125 seconds), and Loveless World (550/126 seconds).

    Why? Skills are mainly activated by seconds, perfects, and notes/combo string.

  • School Idol Skill slot allocations

    Number of School Idol Skill Slots:
    C-Score and O-Score, unidolized: 2 per SR, 3 per SSR, 4 per UR
    C-Score, idolized: 3 per SR, 4 per SSR, 5 per UR
    O-Score, idolized: 4 per SR, 4 per SSR, 5 per UR
